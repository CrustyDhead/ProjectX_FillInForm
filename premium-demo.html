About Us

15 YEARS OF
INDUSTRIAL
EXCELLENCE

Founded in Bratislava in 2009, Brecker Industries has grown from a 12-person welding team to a 200+ engineer industrial assembly company operating across Central Europe.

We work with automotive OEMs, chemical plants, and steel producers who demand precision, reliability, and zero tolerance for delays.

  • ISO 9001:2015 certified quality management system
  • EN 1090 certified steel fabrication to EXC3
  • Own fleet of cranes and heavy lifting equipment
  • Bilingual team — Slovak and German speaking engineers
  • Full project liability insurance up to €5M
